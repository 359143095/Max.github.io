About Me

A journey through code, creativity, and the pursuit of meaningful digital experiences

I'm a passionate web developer and digital storyteller with a love for minimalism and meaningful design. For over a decade, I've been crafting digital experiences that balance functionality with beauty, focusing on the intersection of technology and human connection.

My journey began with a curiosity for how the web works, leading me to explore front-end development, UX design, and the art of digital communication. Today, I specialize in creating immersive, performant websites that tell stories and create genuine connections.

When I'm not coding or writing, you'll find me exploring the outdoors, reading philosophy, or experimenting with new creative tools. I believe that the best digital experiences are rooted in real human experiences, and I bring that perspective to everything I create.
